# Blind Chess Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Blind Chess is a mobile chess training game. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Summary

Blind Chess does not collect, sell, or share personal data with the developer.

Blind Chess does not require account creation, does not include advertising SDKs, and does not use analytics or third-party tracking SDKs.

## Information Handled by the App

Blind Chess may handle the following data locally on your device:

- Practice settings such as voice feedback preferences
- Saved games, imported PGN content, and imported FEN positions
- Clipboard text only when you explicitly use the paste action for FEN or PGN import

This information is used only to provide the app's gameplay and training features on your device.

## Data Collection and Sharing

Blind Chess does not collect personal data and does not transmit your chess data, imported PGN files, imported FEN positions, or settings to the developer.

Blind Chess does not share your data with advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

## On-Device Features

Blind Chess uses on-device text-to-speech features provided by Android to read training cues aloud.

Blind Chess may copy PGN text to the system clipboard when you explicitly use a copy action inside the app.

## Local Storage

Blind Chess stores game-related data and settings locally on your device so that sessions can be resumed and preferences can be remembered.

If your device or Google account uses Android backup or restore features, locally stored app data may be included in those device-level backups under Android system behavior.

## Children's Privacy

Blind Chess is not designed to collect personal information from children or from any other users.
